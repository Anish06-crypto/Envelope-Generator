Envelope Template Generator

Create a custom envelope template to print and enjoy. Put in the desired envelope face dimensions below (remember to measure just the face, not the span from wing to wing) and generate away!

This is what the template design looks like...

This template generator creates a custom envelope template. The way to use the template is up to you - the simplest way is to print on plain paper, cut, fold, and mail away. You can also print on patterned or scrapbook paper or open the file in software such as Photoshop to add graphics and/or a mailing address. Check out some of these creative examples here.

The biggest size for this design that will print on one page of legal paper (8.5" x 11") is 3.9" tall and 5.3" wide. That's going to be the envelope face size, not its dimensions from wing to wing.

As much as I would love to control your printer settings, you will need to turn off scaling and/or fit-to-page options yourself before printing. You can see what I mean here.

Why did I make this? I love writing letters and making cards. They don't always fit nicely into envelopes I have laying around and I'm not good at freehand drawing templates every time.

How can you make your own template? I put the code for this project on Github.
